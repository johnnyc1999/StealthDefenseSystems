Mike McNamara,
10th Degree Black Belt

"I have known John Castaldo for almost fifty years. John was a competitor in karate events from the beginning of the sport through “the golden age of karate”. John was considered then one of the “superstars” of his era and ranked in the top ten fighters in the country. When he retired he became an excellent instructor and his students followed in his competitive footsteps. Having fought several of his students I am well aware of his excellence as an instructor. I would have no qualms in recommending John as a well talented and compassionate teacher. If you have any questions please don’t hesitate to contact me @ 708-473-0682."

Pete Hoffman,
Co-Director, Illinois Martial Arts Hall of Fame

"As the Co- director of the Illinois Martial Arts Hall of Fame, I get to meet some of the finest martial artists in the world. John Castaldo stands out as one of the IMAHF’s greatest inductees. John’s well-earned reputation as the perennial champion and one of the best karate fighters of his era is actually overshadowed by his technical excellence and his abilities as a teacher. As a school owner, John trained countless students who began with a wide range of natural abilities and he produced countless blackbelts, many of whom went on to become top teachers and competitors themselves. A product of his era, John’s expertise did not stop at competition, and he is well known as a topflight, practical, real-world self-defense instructor. I cannot recommend John more highly."
